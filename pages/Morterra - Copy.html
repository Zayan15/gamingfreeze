Morterra

Morterra is an online survival game set at the dawn of man. You awake with absolutely nothing and must find food, water, and protection from others. You will need tools to build a base to protect yourself, armors to defend against attacks, and weapons to fight back. You will eventually grow tired and need to sleep. However, while you sleep you are still vulnerable to attacks, so you must build adequate protection for yourself to survive the night. The world contains no edges, no "safe-zones" (other than bases you build for yourself), and players can construct/destruct at their own will, but be conscious of whose stuff you destroy!

Controls: Use the WASD keys to control player movement. Use the E key to interact with objects in game. Use the TAB key to open/hide your inventory. Use your mouse click to attack. Use the SHIFT key to sprint. Use the V key for voice chat.
